Nexus Health

Established for nearly 10 years, we are committed to offering high quality evidence-based treatments for all musculoskeletal and orthopaedic conditions at out London clinics located in Covent Garden and Northwood.

All our physiotherapists are HCPC registered and are members of the Chartered Society of Physiotherapy.

Our clinics offer comprehensive assessment and goal-orientated treatments for neck and back pains, shoulder injuries, tennis elbow, ankle sprains, knee injuries, tendonitis, sciatica and a host of other sports injuries. We offer Domiciliary physiotherapy within a five-mile radius of our clinics in London for musculoskeletal injuries and post-surgical rehabilitation.

We are also offering Virtual physiotherapy assessment and treatment plan on various online platforms following the current pandemic which is covered by most of the insurance companies
